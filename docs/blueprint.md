# **App Name**: SynergyFlow Digital

## Core Features:

- Interactive Home Page: Display of hero section with 3D animated elements, call-to-action buttons, services overview, client showcases, and parallax effects.
- Engaging About Us Page: An 'About Us' page featuring the company's mission, a team member section, and an animated timeline.
- Service Presentation: A dynamic services page utilizing cards with micro-animations and Lottie icons to showcase digital marketing offerings.
- Filterable Portfolio: A portfolio page with a grid layout that supports filtering to display various project types.
- Firebase Integration: Integration with Firebase Firestore for contact form submissions.
- AI Blog Suggestion: Use an AI tool to suggest relevant blog topics based on current SEO trends and keywords to maintain an active blog.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant purple (#8E44AD) to convey creativity and innovation in digital marketing.
- Background color: A dark navy (#2C3E50) to enhance the vibrant purple and create a modern, tech-savvy feel, with low saturation.
- Accent color: A bright teal (#2ECC71) to highlight call-to-action buttons and important interactive elements. Chosen for high contrast.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font providing a modern and readable experience. Its neutral style suits the technical context, and allows the brighter accent color to take the focus.
- Use a set of modern, minimalist icons that align with the digital marketing industry and brand aesthetics.
- Implement a responsive, grid-based layout that adapts seamlessly to different devices and screen sizes, ensuring a consistent user experience.
- Incorporate subtle 3D animations, hover effects, and scroll-based animations throughout the site to enhance interactivity and visual appeal.
